## About Guntur Red Chilli

Guntur, located in Andhra Pradesh, is world-renowned as the **largest red chilli-producing region in Asia**.  
The region’s unique climate and soil produce chillies known for their **intense heat, deep red color, and powerful aroma**.

Globistix exports a complete range of premium chilli products directly from Guntur farmlands:  
- **Whole Red Chillies (Various Heat Grades)**  
- **Chilli Powder**  
- **Chilli Flakes**  

We ensure **freshness, purity, and international-standard processing**, making our chilli products a preferred choice for global buyers.

## Why Globistix for Red Chilli Exports?

At **Globistix**, we work directly with certified Guntur farmers and processing units.  
Each batch is **sorted, cleaned, stem-cut (if required), sun-dried, crushed, or powdered** under strict hygiene standards.

Our advanced grading and testing make our chilli products suitable for **food manufacturers, spice brands, traders, and importers worldwide**.
