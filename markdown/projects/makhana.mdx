## About Makhana (Fox Nuts)

Makhana, also known as **Fox Nuts** or **Lotus Seeds**, is one of India’s most treasured superfoods. Naturally grown in ponds and wetlands, Makhanas are valued worldwide for their nutritional benefits, unique taste, and versatile uses in the food and wellness industry.

Key qualities of our Makhana exports include:
- **100% Natural & Organic** – free from additives or artificial flavoring  
- **Nutrient-Rich** – high in protein, fiber, calcium, and antioxidants  
- **Versatile Uses** – consumed as healthy snacks, in sweets, curries, or roasted seasoning  
- **Long Shelf Life** – carefully processed and packed to maintain quality  

## Growing Global Demand

With rising awareness of healthy eating, the demand for Makhana has surged in countries like the **USA, UK, UAE, Japan, and Australia**. International markets now recognize it as a sustainable, gluten-free alternative snack — driving continuous growth in exports.
