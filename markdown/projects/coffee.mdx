## About Coorg Coffee

Coorg, located in the lush Western Ghats of Karnataka, is world-famous for its **Arabica and Robusta coffee**.  
Grown in rich soil under ideal climatic conditions, Coorg coffee offers a **distinct balance of aroma, body, and flavor**.  

Globistix exports **premium-quality Coorg coffee beans**, both **green (unroasted)** and **roasted**, to international buyers who value authentic, single-origin Indian coffee.  
We ensure freshness, traceability, and global-standard processing from plantation to port.  



## Why Globistix for Coffee Exports?

At **Globistix**, we partner directly with certified Coorg plantations to source **high-grade Arabica and Robusta beans**.  
Our coffee undergoes meticulous sorting, grading, and quality checks before being processed and packed for export.  

Whether for roasters, traders, or retailers, we deliver coffee that meets **international standards of flavor and consistency**.  

### Key Export Qualities

- **Single-Origin from Coorg** – authentic Indian coffee with full traceability  
- **Green & Roasted Beans** – suitable for both roasters and direct consumers  
- **Distinct Profiles** – Arabica for smooth acidity and aroma; Robusta for strength and crema  
- **Premium Packaging** – customized for bulk or retail shipments  
- **Strict Quality Control** – graded, moisture-tested, and export-ready  


## Global Applications

Globistix supplies Coorg coffee to a wide range of industries and global partners:  

- **Coffee Roasters & Cafés** – single-origin beans for specialty blends and brews  
- **Retail Coffee Brands** – ready-to-sell roasted or packaged green beans  
- **Beverage & Food Manufacturers** – for instant, capsule, or blended coffee products  
- **Distributors & Traders** – bulk shipments for international resale
